Farmer vs Crows is a game with an exciting storyline that will impress both children and adults. Plunge into a world of adventure with many levels.

We all love to shoot with a slingshot, don't we? You have a great opportunity to combine a fun game with a very important mission - to preserve and protect the farmer's harvest from angry birds. Evil crows strive to destroy the entire crop. But you, as the main character, will help a group vegetable of volunteers to prevent their malicious intent.

When playing your favorite game, do not forget that crows are angry and insidious. It is necessary to be extremely collected and attentive in order to shoot accurately with a slingshot. Having chosen one volunteer from vegetables, aim and calculate the flight trajectory so that at the moment of impact inflict maximum destruction on the structures in which the evil and hungry crows are hiding. Remember that the number of volunteers is limited.

At the very beginning, it will be easy for you to adapt and hone your skills in aimed shooting with a slingshot at the constructions that the crows have built to take cover, since angry birds do not expect you to defend the crop! But don't relax and get ready! With each new level, evil crows will build structures of ever larger sizes and it will be more and more difficult to hit them.

They build their fortifications from various materials:
  • boards
  • stones
  • old boxes
  • ice
  • and even cells

In general, everything that was found on the farm. The scale and complexity of the constructions is horrifying at times, but you and your team of volunteers can handle it!

By the way, here is your command:
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Eggplant
  • Onion
  • Carrot

Despite the fact that they look so cute, they are very strong and courageous. They are always ready up to fly and destroy the camp of the angry birds.

Moving along the storyline in the game, you will have to face a system of levels that are divided into five types of difficulty:
  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard
  • Very Hard
  • Hell

Moving on to each new level of difficulty, it will become more and more difficult for you to break structures the first time! The main thing is to build the trajectory correctly, to carefully aim when shooting from a slingshot.

We are confident that you and your team will cope with any difficulties! But if suddenly you have problems with the destruction of the structures of the camp of evil crows, then special skills come to your aid, which you can use at any time! They will help you easily destroy even the most complex structures of the evil raven camp.

And here are these special skills
  • "Earthquake" - causes tremors of the earth from which even the strongest structure will not resist. This merciless natural phenomenon strikes fear in the eyes of all the inhabitants of the raven camp.
  • "Icebreaker" - this skill will help to instantly destroy all the ice that the evil crows used to build their camp.
  • "The key to all locks" - this skill can open and destroy all the cells in which the insidious birds have hidden. This skill is very useful as it is not easy to destroy the cage with the first hit.

We are sure you will enjoy this fun and addicting game. Shoot at slingshot and have fun! Thank you for not staying indifferent and helping the farmer protect his crops from the invasion of angry birds!
